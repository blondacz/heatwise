Install:
  helm dependency update deploy/helm/heatwise-stack
  helm upgrade --install heatwise-stack deploy/helm/heatwise-stack

Kafka:
  * Single-node KRaft (dev). Bootstrap: kafka:9092
  * To persist logs, set `kafka.storage.enabled=true` and a StorageClass.

Heatwise app:
  * Points to kafka:9092 by default. Adjust image repo/tag in values.yaml.
