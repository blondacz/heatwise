Install fresh (recommended when switching to KRaft):
  helm uninstall heatwise-stack || true
  kubectl delete pvc -l app.kubernetes.io/instance=heatwise-stack || true

Then:
  helm dependency update deploy/helm/heatwise-stack
  helm upgrade --install heatwise-stack deploy/helm/heatwise-stack

KRaft clusterId in values.yaml:
  kafka.kraft.clusterId: eXY51YRyw4xolN50glALvQ
Keep this constant across upgrades.
